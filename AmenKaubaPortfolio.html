Dreamweaver - Responsive Entertainment/Business Site

I had to first come out with a idea of what business to use. Once I found out the business that I wanted to use then I had to right down how I wanted the layout and where should everything be placed. After all of that then I was about to start the project and find the layout that I was looking for and placed it inside then to adding the movies. For the last one I did the gallery which just shows some pictures of the other movies that the company was a part of or done. This Project took around 2 to 3 weeks to complete.
